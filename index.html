Track record

Since 2011

  1. 2011 Daralabs founded.
  2. 2012 Incubated at Sahlgrenska Science Park.
  3. 2014 Platform used in SCAPIS Wellness Profiling — part of the SCAPIS study (Swedish Heart-Lung Foundation, six university hospitals).
  4. 2014 Fertility venture begins as Isis (EU trademark registered 2014); ovulation-indicator prototypes 2015–2016; relaunched as MOON Fertility in 2016; wound down 2020.
  5. mid-2010s sEMG rehabilitation R&D begins — the work that became ZiniaCare.
  6. 2016 Service agreement with Sahlgrenska University Hospital for the SCAPIS participant portal.
  7. 2018 Dose Medbox co-founded.
  8. 2018–2022 WearITmed research project (RISE Acreo, with Sahlgrenska Academy, the Swedish School of Textiles and Swerea IVF): Daralabs built the sensor-shirt central electronics unit and the clinical data-collection portal.
  9. 2025 ZiniaCare AB founded — the sEMG rehabilitation venture spun out of Daralabs.
